LA BOCA

La Boca have the rare, impressive ability to create designs and artwork resembling some of the best album sleeves lurking in your dad’s record collection. The studio, founded in 2002, is based in west London and has gathered an envious collection of awards, including Cannes Lions, and the AOI Illustration Awards.

“We work as a studio, so most of our designs are created as a team,” says Bendal. “It's usual to have two or three people working on a single piece.”
